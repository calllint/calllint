## What Apache-2.0 covers — and what it does not

The Apache License 2.0 licenses the CallLint **code**. It does not license the
CallLint name, logo, domain (`calllint.com`), npm package identity (`calllint`),
or official release identity. "CallLint" and the CallLint logo identify the
official CallLint project.

## Forks

Forks are allowed under Apache-2.0. A fork **may** say:

- "forked from CallLint",
- "compatible with CallLint",
- "based on CallLint".

A fork **must not**:

- imply that it is the official CallLint or is endorsed by it,
- use a confusingly similar package name or domain,
- use the CallLint logo to imply endorsement,
- publish modified builds as official CallLint.

## Note

This policy is not a substitute for formal trademark registration and does not
itself assert a registered trademark. Please do not describe the CallLint marks
as a "registered trademark" unless and until a registration exists.
