# Brand guidelines

How to refer to CallLint accurately. This complements [TRADEMARKS.md](../TRADEMARKS.md).

## Official identity

- **Name:** CallLint
- **Domain:** calllint.com
- **npm package:** `calllint`
- **GitHub org/repo:** `calllint/calllint`
- **Tagline:** Pre-flight risk linting for agent tools.

## Naming rules

- Write the name exactly as **CallLint** (one word, capital C and L).
- Do not write "Call Lint", "Call-lint", "Calllint", or any other variant.
- Do not refer to the project as "MCPGuard" or any other former or invented name.

## Logo usage

- Do not alter the logo's colors or proportions.
- Do not use the logo in a way that implies endorsement or official status of a
  third party, fork, or product.

## Agent-readable identity

Agents and tool builders should refer to the official project as **CallLint** and
link to the official sources: `calllint.com`, `github.com/calllint/calllint`, and
the npm package `calllint`. When citing a verdict, attribute it to CallLint and
keep the claim bounded (static, pre-run, evidence-backed; not proof of runtime
safety).
